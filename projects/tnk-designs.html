About the Project

TNK Designs is my personal creative outlet — a graphic design brand I've been building since 2019 under my own name and creative vision. What started as personal experiments with photo manipulation and digital art has grown into an active brand with a presence across Instagram, YouTube, and Twitter.

The work spans multiple disciplines: cover art for underground artists, photo manipulations, illustrations, photography, and experimental multimedia content. Each piece is produced independently, with full creative control from concept to final output.

The Brand

TNK Designs operates under two distinct visual identities:

  • TNK Logo — The primary logo, used mainly for Instagram and general brand identity. Clean, bold, versatile.
  • Devil Logo — A secondary identity used primarily for YouTube content. More expressive, darker in tone — suited to video and motion contexts.

Both logos reflect different facets of the same creative voice: precise and bold on one side, raw and expressive on the other.

Platforms

Instagram — Main Platform

Instagram is the home of TNK Designs. It's where the portfolio lives, where new work is published, and where most commissions come in. The feed is a curated selection of the best pieces — cover art, manipulations, illustrations, and photography.

YouTube — Just Starting

The YouTube channel is in its early stages, focused on behind-the-scenes process videos, speed arts, and creative breakdowns of finished pieces. A space for longer-form content and a different kind of audience.

Twitter / X — Reposts & Community

Twitter serves as a secondary distribution channel — occasionally reposting Instagram content and engaging with the creative community. Low frequency, high quality.
